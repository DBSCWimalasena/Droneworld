Potensic A20 Mini Drone(2022)

Specifications

Drone Size and Weight

This drone comes in a small and lightweight design, measuring 3.5 by 3.1 inches and weighing less than 100 grams. It fits comfortably in your palm and is perfect for flying indoors and outdoors.

Drone Battery

The Potensic Mini Drone A20 RC Nano Quadcopter comes with a non-removable battery that provides about 15 minutes of flying time when fully charged. Charging takes approximately 40 minutes with the provided USB charging cable.

Safety Features

The drone features an alarm to alert you when it's time to fly it home and when it's approaching the flight range limit. However, it does not have an automatic return to home feature.

Flight

Flying this quadcopter is easy with features like a single take-off button, Altitude Hold mode, speed adjustment, and headless mode for directional control.

The Controller

The controller is easy to use with responsive joysticks, although it has a short range. It requires 3 AAA batteries for operation.
